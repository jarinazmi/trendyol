Who we are?

Business Info
Since 2010, our mission is to make a positive impact by offering seamless e-commerce experience to customers and sellers.
The trust of over 30 million customers and 260 thousand sellers propelled us to become the first decacorn in Turkey and one of the top e-commerce platforms in the world.
Giving back to the community is in our DNA. We bring this into action by supporting the digitalization of our sellers, empowering local manufacturers and advocating women's participation in the digital economy and production.
We maintain our ecosystem's leading R&D center, Trendyol Tech. With Trendyol Express we operate one of the most rapidly developing logistics networks and with Trendyol Go we are more than a fashion destination in Turkey, offering an instant food delivery service. With Dolap, one of the largest pre-owned goods platforms, we do our best to contribute to a circular economy and sustainability.

We’re in this for the long-run and we’d love for you to be part of this journey.
